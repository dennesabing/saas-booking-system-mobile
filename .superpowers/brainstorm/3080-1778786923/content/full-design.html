Design summary

  • Background: Sky-to-mint gradient (light) / deep navy gradient (dark)
  • Cards & surfaces: rgba(255,255,255,0.6) frosted glass (light) / rgba(255,255,255,0.06) dark glass
  • Accent: Sky blue #0ea5e9 (light) / #38bdf8 (dark)
  • Tab icons: Calendar (Bookings) + Person (Profile) — outlined inactive, filled active
  • Theme switcher: 🌙 label + iOS-style toggle in Profile header card
  • Theme context: ThemeContext with tokens, persisted to expo-secure-store
